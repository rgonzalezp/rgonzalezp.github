Ricardo's Philosophy of life

  • Now you are probably thinking...
    "Who the hell is this guy?" As you saw in the title....

    I am a computer scientist studying in the University of Los Andes,Colombia. I aspire to become a person that can help a lot of people and teach them how willpower and persistence can achieve great things.
    Cool fact about me: I have triple nationalities. Spanish, Venezuelan and Colombian.
  • My Philosophy is very simple as you see in the picture. There's no point in worrying/complaining almost at any instance in your life. You are using your very precious and limited time on something that won't achieve anything and most likely won't help you resolve the issue. Use your time to it's fullest potential and enjoy everything you do with it. It's valid to have regrets, but try to make them as rare as possible
    Value other's people work genuinely. When you appreciate things created by other people, you feel a sense of duty that gives you purpose... one of the strongest drivers to any human being.
  • Try your hardest
  • Feel good about the things you do
  • Don't waste time
  • Appreciate other's people effort sincerely
  • Care for other's as the opportunity arise
